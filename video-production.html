Production requirements

Depending on the type of project, you may need more or fewer steps than what are listed here.

Note: For most projects, you will need access to high-end video and audio equipment.

  1. Synopsis
    • write a short synopsis of the video work.
  2. Storyboarding
    • Storyboarder App
    • Storyboarding for film and animation
  3. Script
    • Scrivener
    • Final Draft
  4. Sound recording
    • Sound foley
    • Recording narration and actor dialogue
  5. Scoring
    • Pro Tools
    • GarageBand
    • Premiere Pro
  6. Effects, editing, compositing, rendering
    • Effects
      • Adobe AfterEffects
    • Editing (NLE)
      • Premiere Pro
      • DaVinci Resolve
    • Compositing
      • Natron
      • Nuke
      • Blender compositing
